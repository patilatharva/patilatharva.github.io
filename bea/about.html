About us

BEA's mission is to be the comprehensive guide and helper for everything happening at UC Berkeley related to entrepreneurship.

Our goals

01

Short term

Equip our members with the skills, network, support, and knowledge necessary to launch or grow a venture. Host special events throughout the year with the goal of enriching the entrepreneurial spirit at UC Berkeley. Enable our members to network with leading startups and venture capitalists.

02

Long term

Create a national/international network between universities and students to share resources, events and skills across campuses.
Take advantage of niche areas and specialties from every geography and student area.
