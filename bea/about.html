About us

BEA is your one-stop-shop for everything happening at UC Berkeley related to entrepreneurship.

Our mission

01

Accessibility

Creating a bridge of information and resources between entrepreneurs in UC Berkeley and outside institutions.

02

Diversity

Nurturing a collaborative ecosystem for entrepreneurs with various skills and perspectives.

03

Networking

Expanding our entrepreneurial network to allow dynamic interactions such as job finding and investment opportunities.

04

Food

Providing free foods and drinks!

Join us

Students are welcome to join at any stage of their ideation, development, fundraising or expansion, from any industry. If you are interested in learning more about how to be an entrepreneur, bring your idea to life and meet other like minded, ambitious people on campus, we’d love for you to join us!
