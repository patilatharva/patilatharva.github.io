About Me

Hey there, I'm Atharva!

I'm an undergraduate student at the University of California, Berkeley pursuing a double major in Computer Science and Statistics and on track to graduate in 2022.

Some of my current interests are: exploring the intersection of Computer Science and Urban Studies—specifically, how to apply technologies like machine learning in Intelligent Transportation Systems, networking in the tech-for-social-good startup and venture capital scene in Silicon Valley, meeting people through soccer and enjoying Japanese cuisine.
